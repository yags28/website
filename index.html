01 About

Engineering meets purpose.

I'm a robotics and mechatronics engineer based in West Lafayette, Indiana, pursuing an MS in Robotics. My work spans autonomous systems, computer vision, and embedded control — from PLC-driven industrial automation to ROS 2 mobile robots.

My grandfather suffered a subdural hematoma that erased his memory entirely. Watching that shaped my research focus: I'm driven to build robotic systems for neurorehabilitation — exoskeletons for post-hematoma recovery and precision platforms for stem cell delivery to the brain.

Outside the lab, I've trekked Kedarkantha, taught underprivileged kids interview skills with an NGO, and I never miss football on weekends.
